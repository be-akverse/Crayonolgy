The Story

Making fonts isnt my thing, but making Websites is! Crayonology was born where the goal was simple: spend as many hours as possible making a font.

Every letter was drawn with thick marker strokes, wobbly lines, and the unfiltered joy of a toddler who just discovered that walls are also paper.

Use it for birthday cards, chaotic posters, or that one design client who said "make it fun but not too fun." This is too fun.

Hackathon Stats:
Hours spent: 4+ | Letters drawn: 52 | Crayons broken: 0 (they were markers)

Made with pure passion for an E-READER :)
